Trader Joe's Cannoli Dip with Waffle Chips

Description:

This is a great recipe for Trader Joe's Cannoli Dip with Waffle Chips that can be made easily and quickly with only a few ingredients.

Ingredients for the recipe:

  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Waffle chips (or other dippers of your choice)

Steps to make the recipe:

  1. Combine the ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, mini chocolate chips, and vanilla extract in a mixing bowl.
  2. Stir until well combined and creamy.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a serving bowl.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve with waffle chips or other dippers of your choice.
